About Pacific Trails Resort

Pacific Trails Resort was founded with one goal in mind: to bring people closer to nature while offering the comfort of a relaxing retreat. Our resort rests along California’s scenic coast, where every sunrise and sunset tells its own story.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create meaningful stays for every guest. We believe that peace, adventure, and nature should all exist in harmony, helping people reconnect with what truly matters.

Our Values

  • Respect for nature and community
  • Comfort through simplicity
  • Memorable hospitality

Our Team

Our friendly team works year-round to make sure your experience is special. From the moment you arrive until your last hike along the coast.
